Requested
A task raised by an internal stakeholder, or an idea for a product update that has been logged for consideration. Requested tasks should be moved to Backlog or Roadmap as soon as possible — Backlog for anything near-term (within the next month), Roadmap for anything realistically further away than that.
Backlog
A task that has been selected and is scheduled to be worked on in the near future.
In progress
A task that is currently being actively worked on by a designer.
On hold
A task that was in progress but has been paused, typically due to a dependency, change in priority, or awaiting input.
Review

Handover

Why am I seeing ClickUp on screen?
ClickUp is the platform we use to manage our tasks. The views embedded here pull directly from our live ClickUp workspace.
Active

The Active view shows tasks currently in progress or coming up soon. Each task can display up to three dates:

  • Start date — when work on the task begins.
  • Review date — the point at which the work requires internal discussion before it can move forward.
  • Due date — the target completion date.

Design work is iterative and often requires multiple review sessions. For more complex tasks, a due date may not be set until a critical mass of agreement has been reached through those reviews. For more straightforward work, a review date may be less necessary, with a due date entered directly.

Roadmap
A list of ideas that are not currently considered a near-term priority and fall outside the coming month's scope of work. These are things worth keeping track of for the future.
Submit a request
A simple form that allows anyone to submit a design request directly to the design team.
