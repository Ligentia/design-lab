UX maturity survey

Ligentia design team · Annual benchmark · v4

19 questions · ~10 minutes

This survey helps us understand how design work is perceived and integrated across the organisation. Your answers are used to track our progress and identify where we can improve.

The survey takes around 10 minutes. Some questions may not reflect your day-to-day experience with the design team — where that’s the case, please select Not applicable rather than guessing. These responses are just as useful: they help us understand where our work isn’t visible across the organisation.

There are no right or wrong answers.
